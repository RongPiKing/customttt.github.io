CustomTTT: Motion and Appearance Customized Video Generation via Test-Time Training

Presented at AAAI 2025

In this work, we introduce CustomTTT, a novel method for customizing video generation by combining motion patterns from reference videos and appearance features from images. The approach utilizes Test-Time Training (TTT) to achieve a seamless blend of motion and appearance, enabling high-quality video synthesis with fine-grained control over the movement and visual characteristics. Our results demonstrate the effectiveness of CustomTTT in applications ranging from entertainment to virtual reality and personalized content generation.
