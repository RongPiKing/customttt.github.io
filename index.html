Abstract

The task of customizing video generation involves learning the motion and appearance aspects from different reference sources, which is traditionally a challenging task. In this paper, we introduce CustomTTT, a novel method that combines Test-Time Training (TTT) to generate customized videos. Our method takes advantage of motion patterns from reference videos and appearance characteristics from images to create new videos that combine these features. The key advantage of our approach lies in the ability to generate realistic videos with fine-grained motion control and appearance customization, enabling applications in entertainment, virtual reality, and personalized content creation.
